Who We Are &
Why We Build.

Subtotal Plus is more than a construction company. We're a team of passionate builders, designers, and managers committed to transforming Uganda's built environment — one project at a time.

Our Story

Built from the Ground Up.

Subtotal Plus was founded with a simple but powerful belief — that Uganda deserves world-class construction. We started small, taking on residential projects in Kampala, and grew steadily through a reputation for quality, honesty, and reliability.

Over a decade later, we've evolved into a full-service construction and development company with a portfolio spanning residential homes, commercial buildings, large-scale estates, and everything in between.

Today, Subtotal Plus operates as the flagship construction business of Subtotal Plus Enterprise — a growing multi-sector company headquartered in Kampala.
